The Knight

Bio

The Knight is the main protagonist of Hollow Knight, a mysterious silent warrior exploring the ruined kingdom of Hallownest. With a simple design, the Knight’s purpose and identity are a central part of the game’s narrative, shrouded in mystery. The Knight’s journey is filled with danger, discovery, and encounters with various characters that play key roles in unraveling the secrets of Hallownest. As the player progresses, they uncover more about the Knight's past and their connection to the kingdom's downfall...

SPELLS

Spells are skills the Knight can activate by either holding FOCUS/CAST (Focus) or tapping FOCUS/CAST or QUICK CAST while neutral (Vengeful Spirit/Shade Soul) or while holding UP (Howling Wraiths/Abyss Shriek) or DOWN (Desolate Dive/Descending Dark).

Focus

Focus collected SOUL to repair your shell and heal damage.

  • Strike enemies to gather SOUL.
  • Hold FOCUS/CAST

Vengeful Spirit

Conjure a spirit that will fly forward and burn foes in its path.

  • The spirit requires SOUL to be conjured. Strike enemies to gather SOUL.
  • FOCUS/CAST or QUICK CAST

Desolate Dive

Strike the ground with a concentrated force of SOUL. This force can destroy foes or break through fragile structures.

  • The force requires SOUL to be conjured. Strike enemies to gather SOUL.
  • DOWN + CAST

Howling Wraiths

Blast foes with screaming SOUL.

  • The Wraiths requires sic SOUL to be conjured. Strike enemies to gather SOUL.
  • UP + CAST

Shade Soul

Conjure a shadow that will fly forward and burn foes in its path.

  • The shadow requires SOUL to be conjured. Strike enemies to gather SOUL.
  • CAST

Descending Dark

Strike the ground with a concentrated force of SOUL and Shadow. This force can destroy foes or break through fragile structures.

  • The force requires SOUL to be conjured. Strike enemies to gather SOUL.

ABILITIES

Abilities are skills the Knight can find throughout the game that make traversing Hallownest easier.

Mothwing Cloak

Cloak threaded with mothwing strands. Allows the wearer to dash forwards along the ground or through the air.

  • Dash forward a short distance. The cooldown between dashes is 0.6 seconds
  • DASH

Mantis Claw

Claw carved from bone. Allows the wearer to cling to walls and leap off them.

  • Wall slide and wall jump
  • LEFT/RIGHT against a wall JUMP while sliding

Crystal Heart

The energy core of an old mining golem, fashioned around a potent crystal. The crystal's energy can be channeled sic to launch the bearer forward at dangerous speeds.

  • Fly horizontally
  • Hold and release SUPER DASH

Isma's Tear

Fruit formed from a single hardened tear. Once ingested it provides protection from the searing, acidic pools found in certain parts of Hallownest.

  • Swim in acid
  • -

Shade Cloak

Cloak formed from the substance of the Abyss. Allows the wearer to dash through enemies and their attacks without taking damage.

  • Dash through damage and Shade Gates
  • DASH

Dream Nail

Allows the wielder to cut through the veil between dreams and waking. Can be used to reveal hidden dreams or open gateways.

  • Collect Essence and enter dreams
  • DREAM NAIL

Awoken Dream Nail

Allows the wielder to cut through the veil between dreams and waking. Can be used to reveal hidden dreams or open gateways.

  • Collect Essence and enter dreams
  • DREAM NAIL

NAIL ARTS

Nail Arts are abilities using the Nail, taught to the Knight by the Nailmasters. Executing a Nail Art involves holding down ATTACK to charge, then releasing while doing an optional second input depending on the Nail Art.

Cyclone Slash

The signature Nail Art of Nailmaster Mato. A spinning attack that rapidly strikes foes on all sides..

Dash Slash

TThe signature Nail Art of Nailmaster Oro. Strike ahead quickly after dashing forward.

Great Slash

The signature Nail Art of Nailmaster Sheo. Unleashes a huge slash directly in front of you which deals extra damage to foes.
